Ever wondered what the Devil would say in his TED Talk? Well let’s have a look into Lucifer Morningstar’s TED Talk,
“Awake, arise or be forever fall’n”

1. To be heard you must first listen.
(Lucifer heavily places faith in what others have to say. He validates his own ideas by listening to his friends’ opinions.)

...

2. To be truthful to oneself and others.
(Lucifer honors truth above anything else, he believes being extremely honest no matter how many times a lie presents itself and this is a quality we all should possess.)

3. A life lived for others a life worthwhile.
(While he does still put himself in most focus, Lucifer always goes out of his way to help his friends. He willingly listened to Ella’s problems and always took care and protected Chloe.)

4. Self-sacrifice enables us to sacrifice others without blushing.
(This can be evidenced by his selfless act of returning to Hell to protect people on Earth from demons.)

5. You have the power to forge your own path.
(Have the courage to break the norms and follow your passion, as Lucifer redefines himself and does not do what people expect from the ruler of hell.)

Ever wondered what everyone’s favourite Naruto Uzumaki would say in his TED Talk? Well let’s have a look,

Naruto is not exactly well known for making great life choices. In fact, we don’t call him a knucklehead for nothing!

However, there is a lot we can learn from his mistakes:...
1. Sticking up for someone is always the right move, even if it doesn’t seem like a fight you can win.
(Naruto stood up for Hinata against bullies, even though he was outnumbered and outmatched).
2. Its always good to trust and follow your instincts.
(On Naruto’s mission to the Land of Birds with Neji and TenTen, his instincts about the case not being solved so neatly, were right).
3. Something’s just aren’t meant to be.
(Naruto’s experience with the Bikochu bug).
4. Spend time with your family, because no matter what the situation, your family will always be there.
(Boruto feels that his father, Naruto isn’t home to spend time with him, so much so that he goes ahead to make shadow clones).
5. Know your Strengths (And your Weaknesses)
(Naruto, not wanting to be outdone and jealous that no one is challenging Rock Lee, attacks him before Sasuke can even face off and Rock Lee thoroughly dispatches Naruto, barely breaking a sweat.)
