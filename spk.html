Speakers


Darren Hood

Principal UX Designer


Darren Hood has been involved in user experience design operations for over 20 years. He has been as a full-time UX professional for 16+ years. He has previously worked for the Fortune 50 and for some of the largest digital design agencies in the world. He currently serves as an adjunct professor for Kent State University's UX Design Master's program and Lawrence Technological University. He is also the host for The World of UX podcast on CX of M radio via Michigan State University and was elected to serve as the Director of Education for UXPA International.

Rahul Aggarwal

Founder at Mission Oxygen,
Co-Founder - Designhill


Rahul Aggarwal is an entrepreneur, investor and marketer who in 2014 co-founded one of the largest creative marketplaces, Designhill, which has over 125,000 artists and designers from all over the world. He along with a community of entrepreneurs and founders from Delhi-NCR started Mission Oxygen – an initiative helped with the oxygen crisis in India during the second wave of Covid19. Mission oxygen has donated over 5200 oxygen concentrators to over 375 hospitals across 26 states in India and is setting up 30+ oxygen plants, crowdsourcing more than 50 crore rupees from 38000+ people all around the world.

Dr. Rishi Raj

Associate Professor - IIT Patna


Dr. Rishi Raj is an Associate Professor at the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Patna. Prior to this, he was a Postdoctoral Associate at the Device Research Laboratory in the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). With five patents and more than 50 research papers published, Dr Rishi has made significant contributions to the fields of Energy, Boiling Condensation, Micro-/Nano-Scale Thermal and Fluidic Transport, Colloids and Interface Science, and Microgravity Science. He has also been a recipient of the Medal for Young Scientist which is awarded by the Indian National Science Academy, and a recipient of INAE Young Engineer Award.

Vivek Ram, Vaanarsena

CG and VFX Artist


Vivek Ram, also known as The Senapati of Vaanarsena Studios, has had a career span of 20 years as a CG and VFX Artist. He is responsible for building high quality character and environment models as modeling technical director for international feature films like Superman Returns, Fast and the Furious - Tokyo Drift, Night at the Museum, The Incredible Hulk and many more. His incredible interest in ancient Indian stories led him to his new venture, Vaanarsena Studios. He believes that Indian mythology is very open to interpretation and his artwork has been successful in giving new dimensions to the stories that we have been hearing for ages.

Yusra Mardini

Swimmer, Olympian


Yusra Mardini is an Olympic swimmer currently living in Hamburg, Germany. She was born in Syria in 1998. She competed in the 2016 Rio Olympics and Tokyo Olympics 2020 under the Olympics flag, as a member of the Refugee Olympic Athletes Team.Yusra previously represented Syria in international competitions. She resumed her training after moving to Berlin in 2015 and qualified for Olympics in 2016, being one of the ten athletes selected for the Refugee Olympic Team. In April 2017, Yusra (19 y/o) was appointed as a Goodwill Ambassador for UNHCR, the UN Refugee Agency. She advocates for refugees globally through sharing her own inspiring story and has become a powerful voice for the forcibly displaced across the world.

Rupesh Mahore

Researcher & Innovator


Rupesh Mahore is currently working as a remote research intern at Harvard University with his study being focussed on Molecular Dynamics Simulations. He has also worked with Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) during the Chandrayaan 2 mission. He has also worked with Padma Shri awarded Prof HC Verma in his project: Shiksha Sopan at IIT Kanpur.He has also invented an oxygen conservation, optimisation, and monitoring device: Oxyserve for COVID-19 patients which could save lives of millions of people suffering from COPDs. Rupesh has also been selected for the prestigious CERN - Entrepreneurship Program 2021.

Captain Zoya Agarwal

Aircraft Pilot, UN Spokesperson, TEDx Speaker


Captain Zoya Agarwal is an Indian commercial pilot flying for Air India. She commanded the all-women cockpit crew that scripted aviation history in January 2021 by operating the longest non-stop commercial flight by any airline to India, flying from San Francisco to Bengaluru. In the year 2013, She became the youngest female pilot to fly a Boeing 777. She is also a TEDx speaker and aims to inspire young and aspiring female pilots and youth by motivating them on various platforms. In August 2021, she was chosen by the United Nations as its spokesperson for Generation Equality.
